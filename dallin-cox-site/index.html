About

I'm Dallin Cox, Director of Operations at Retina and Vitreous Surgeons of Utah, where I oversee four clinics, 25+ clinical and administrative staff, and 1,000+ monthly patient visits across Salt Lake County, Utah County, and rural outreach sites.

My path into healthcare administration started as a medical assistant — I saw an opportunity to take on operational responsibilities and never looked back. I've since built a track record of turning operational problems into measurable results: $100K+ in annual supply chain savings, $50K+ from reimbursement renegotiation, and a 40% increase in website traffic after bringing digital marketing in-house.

I'm especially interested in the intersection of healthcare operations and advanced analytics — building systems, models, and tools that help organizations make faster, smarter decisions. I leverage AI heavily in my work and believe it's the defining skill set for the next generation of healthcare leaders.
